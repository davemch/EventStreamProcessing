Appeal Monitoring

If the amount of Mentions increases by more than 10% comparing to the previous week, a warning is shown

Accusation Monitoring

If the amount of Mentions increases by more than 60% comparing to the previous week, a warning is shown

Refuse Monitoring

If the amount of Mentions increases by more than 10% comparing to the previous week, a warning is shown

Escalation Monitoring

If the amount of Mentions increases by more than 10% comparing to the previous week, a warning is shown

Eruption Monitoring

If the amount of Mentions increases by more than 20% comparing to the previous week, a warning is shown

Unrest Monitoring

If more than 3 warnings are triggered within a week, this indicates a Social Unrest Event
